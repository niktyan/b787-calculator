# B787 Tools — Support

Thank you for using B787 Tools, an offline advisory crosswind suite for Boeing 787 pilots.

## How to get help

For questions, bug reports, feature requests, or general feedback:

**Email:** [supportb787calculator@protonmail.com](mailto:supportb787calculator@protonmail.com)

The developer is an active Boeing 787 line pilot. Responses typically arrive within 1–3 days.

## Common questions

### How do I report incorrect crosswind values?

If you find a value that disagrees with your FCOM/QRH, please email with:

1. The exact inputs you used (aircraft, weight, CG, runway condition, mode).
2. The value the app showed.
3. The value you expected (with FCOM page/section reference if possible).

We validate all data with active line pilots, but corrections are always welcome.

### Does the app collect any data?

No. The app makes zero network requests. All settings and your last 20 calculations are stored locally on your device. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.html) for the full statement.

### Why is the app advisory only?

All values are conservative reference numbers. They are designed to give pilots a quick first pass before consulting the FCOM. They do not replace official Boeing documentation, certified EFB software, or your operator's procedures. See our [Terms of Use](terms-of-use.html) for full details.

### Does it work offline?

Yes, completely. The app needs no internet connection at any time. All calculations run on-device.

### Will more modules be added?

Possibly — Weight & Balance, Performance V-speeds, and Fuel Planning are on the roadmap. Apple Watch companion is under consideration.
